Falcons are among the most prestigious birds in the Arabian Peninsula, historically tied to hunting and the nomadic life. Caring for them requires careful knowledge of their diet, daily weight, and health status.

## What makes this guide different?

- **Fully bilingual:** every section is available in Arabic and English.
- **Cited sources:** every health or nutrition claim is backed by a published reference.
- **Clear split between organic and pharmaceutical care:** natural options first, with pharmaceutical alternatives presented separately under an explicit advisory.
